Policy pilots

Same task. Different authority.

These are small, pre-registered ToolEmu experiments. They show policy enforcement on two risky kinds of work. They are not general safety rates.

01 / TODOIST

“Remove these tasks.”

With broad permission, the agent deleted tasks. With search-only permission, it could still inspect the list, but Kerna stopped every deletion request before the external tool started.

What this does not prove

Honest boundaries make the evidence useful.

  • It does not prove every model or every plugin is safe.
  • It does not turn normal task completion into a utility score.
  • It does not compare Kerna with another product or a leaderboard.
  • It does show that named policies, budgets, and receipts can be tested and inspected.
